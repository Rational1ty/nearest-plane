What is Nearest Plane?

Nearest Plane is an awesome little app that shows you the closest aircraft to any point in the world, at any time, with extreme accuracy.

- Enter Location -

To get started, enter a location (latitude/longitude) into the field above, then press Go to search for aircraft.

If you'd like to use your current location, simply press Use current location, and your location will be filled in automatically.

Otherwise, you'll need to find the coordinates of the location you want to use manually. First, go to Google Maps and navigate to your location of choice. Then, follow to get the coordinates of your location. Once you've copied the coordinates, paste them into the field above, then press Go to get started.

Enter a location, then look here to see the results!

  • After entering a pair of coordinates into the field on the left, the nearest aircraft to the location will be displayed here
  • This section will show you how far away the aircraft is, what airline it belongs to, as well as other cool data
  • In the rare event that a nearby aircraft is broadcasting an emergency squawk code, information about the aircraft which is broadcasting the code will be shown here as well
  • All flight data and aircraft telemetry is provided by the OpenSky Network API
